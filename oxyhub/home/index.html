Key Features

User Profiles and Feeds

  • User profiles similar to Twitter, where users can post updates, photos, videos, and other media.

  • A feed algorithm to prioritize content based on relevance and popularity within the user's interests.

Communities and Sub-communities

  • Ability to create and join communities (similar to subreddits) focused on specific topics, interests, or themes.

  • Each community can have its own moderators, rules, and customizations, similar to Reddit.

Real-time Chat and Messaging

  • Integrated chat functionality similar to Discord, allowing users to communicate with each other in real-time through text, voice, and video.

  • Option for public channels and private messaging, with moderation tools for community leaders.

Content Discovery and Recommendations

  • Explore page featuring trending topics, popular posts, and recommended communities based on user preferences and activity.

  • Personalized recommendations for users to discover new content and communities based on their interests and interactions.

Multimedia Support

  • Support for various types of media including images, videos, GIFs, and links.

  • Advanced media sharing options, such as embedding media directly into posts and comments.

Moderation and Community Management

  • Tools for community moderators to manage content, enforce rules, and moderate discussions.

  • Reporting and flagging system for users to notify moderators of inappropriate content or behavior.

Customization and Personalization

  • Customizable user profiles and community pages with options for themes, banners, and layouts.

  • Personalization features such as customizable feed preferences and notification settings.

[BETA] Integration with External Platforms

  • Integration with external services and platforms, such as Twitter, YouTube, and Spotify, allowing users to share content seamlessly across platforms.

  • API support for third-party developers to build integrations and extensions for the platform.

Accessibility and Privacy

  • Accessibility features to ensure the platform is usable for users with disabilities.

  • Robust privacy settings giving users control over their data, visibility, and interactions.

Cross-platform Compatibility

  • Availability on multiple platforms including web, iOS, Android, and desktop applications.

  • Synchronization of user data and preferences across devices for a seamless user experience.

Analytics and Insights

  • Analytics tools for users and community moderators to track engagement, audience demographics, and other metrics.

  • Insights into trending topics, popular content formats, and community growth patterns.

Search and Discovery

  • Advanced search functionality allowing users to find specific posts, communities, or users.

  • Filters and sorting options for search results based on relevance, popularity, and recency.
