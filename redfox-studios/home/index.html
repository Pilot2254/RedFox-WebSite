Team RedFox is a community of passionate gamers who share a love of gaming. Our team of dedicated gamers and developers are committed to providing our users with the best gaming experience possible. RedFox was born in 2023 and was founded by a gamer named Pilot2254, who is an avid gamer himself.

RedFox Developer Program

The RFDP (RedFox Develop Program) service was created for beginners and advanced programmers in any programming language. Our application is your gateway to mastering programming languages in an interactive and community-driven environment. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, RFDP provides a dynamic platform to learn, collaborate, and build real-world skills. It's like duolingo, but with programming languages! You can learn to program from senior programmers, chat, talk on the phone with others, or just relax.

StrikeForce

StrikeForce stands as a thrilling testament to the evolution of competitive gaming, blending the best elements of Valorant and CS:GO into a singular, pulse-pounding experience. At its core, StrikeForce is a 5v5 battleground where players engage in strategic warfare, each match a symphony of skill, strategy, and split-second decision-making. Like its predecessors, it demands not only sharp aim and quick reflexes but also cunning tactics and meticulous teamwork. Whether orchestrating coordinated assaults, holding key positions, or executing precise flanking maneuvers, every moment in StrikeForce is charged with the potential for triumph or defeat. With its diverse array of meticulously crafted maps and a vibrant community of passionate gamers, StrikeForce offers an unparalleled gaming experience where every player has the chance to leave their mark on the competitive landscape.
