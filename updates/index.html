So what is happening is that Team RedFox is no longer an official company, it is changing to a project and this project will be owned by the company RedFox Studios. RedFox Studios will be like a "mother" and Team RedFox will be the child of this mother. As well as StrikeForce, OxyHub and other projects that used to belong to Team RedFox will now belong to RedFox Studios.

From September I'll start at the new school and there I'll work on StrikeForce (a game), and I'll work on other projects as well. Why did i changed the "mother" of those projects? Well, it seemed weird to me that all the projects and things fall under the gaming community, so I created RedFox Studios, which is actually a studio that works on those projects. And our goal in the future will be to create a big company that will work with other companies, invest in them and help them on their projects. This is actually RedFox Studios.
