About StrikeForce

StrikeForce is an adrenaline-pumping FPS game that combines the best elements of tactical shooters like CS2 and the fast-paced action of Warface: Clutch. Engage in intense firefights, execute precise strategies, and dominate the battlefield with your team.

Game Features

StrikeForce offers a rich, immersive gameplay experience with a variety of features including diverse agent roles, dynamic battlefields, and engaging game modes. Dive into the action and discover the depth of strategic combat that awaits you.

Precision Gunplay

Team-based Tactics

Skill Progression

Elite Agents

Choose from five unique agents, each bringing their own set of skills and tactical advantages to the battlefield:

  • Assault: Front-line combat specialist
  • Engineer: Tech expert and fortification master
  • Sniper: Long-range precision eliminator
  • Medic: Vital support and healing expert
  • A.T.A.I.: Advanced AI with unparalleled tactical analysis

Master each agent's abilities to turn the tide of battle and secure victory for your team.

Dynamic Battlefields

Engage in intense combat across a variety of meticulously designed maps, each offering unique strategic challenges:

  • Urban Warfare: Navigate tight corners and vertical combat in city environments
  • Desert Storm: Master long-range engagements in open terrain
  • Arctic Base: Adapt to harsh conditions in a secretive polar facility
  • Jungle Ops: Use dense foliage for stealth and ambush tactics

Every map is crafted to test your skills and teamwork, ensuring no two battles feel the same.

Thrilling Game Modes

StrikeForce offers a variety of game modes to suit every playstyle:

PvP Modes

  • Team Deathmatch (TD): Classic team-based combat
  • Free For All (FFA): Every agent for themselves in all-out warfare
  • Capture: Secure and hold objectives to score points

PvP matches are intense 15-minute battles, perfect for quick, action-packed gaming sessions.

PvE Mode

Experience the thrill of cooperative gameplay in our story-driven PvE missions:

  • Solo or team up with up to 5 players
  • Face off against advanced AI enemies
  • Progress through challenging rooms and scenarios
  • Earn exclusive rewards upon completion

PvE missions typically last 20-30 minutes, offering a deeper, more immersive StrikeForce experience.
