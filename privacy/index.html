Privacy Policy

Last updated: [5/10/2023]

1. Introduction

Welcome to Team RedFox ("we," "us," or "our"). We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and securing your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to comprehensively explain how we gather, utilize, share, and protect your data when you engage with our website, avail yourself of our services, or interact with us in any manner. By accessing or utilizing our services, you unequivocally consent to the practices detailed herein.


2. Information We Collect

We may collect a variety of information, including:

Personal Information: This includes data such as your name, email address, contact information, and any other identifiers you voluntarily provide.

Usage Information: We gather data regarding your interactions with our website and services, encompassing pages viewed, features used, and the duration of your visits.

Device Information: Information related to your device, encompassing but not limited to your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We utilize cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience, assess trends, and obtain information about your navigation patterns on our website.


3. How We Use Your Information

We employ your information for a spectrum of purposes, including but not limited to:

Service Delivery and Enhancement: To facilitate, maintain, and augment our services, personalize your user experience, and address any technical glitches or malfunctions.

Communication: To keep you informed about updates, notifications, newsletters, and promotional materials related to our services.

Legal Compliance: To adhere to legal obligations and protect our rights, interests, and the overall security of our services.

Research and Analytics: To analyze user behavior, preferences, and trends, thereby aiding us in fine-tuning our offerings to cater to your evolving needs.


4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

Service Providers: Trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating and maintaining our services. Rest assured, they adhere to stringent privacy and security standards.

Legal Authorities: We may disclose your information when required by law, or to safeguard our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, your information may be conveyed to another entity, provided they assume the same commitments towards safeguarding your data.


5. Your Choices

You wield significant control over your information:

Access and Update: You can access, correct, or update your personal information within our systems.

Marketing Communications: Should you wish to cease receiving marketing emails from us, simply follow the opt-out instructions embedded within the email.

Cookies: You possess the ability to manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings.


6. Security

We diligently implement robust security measures to protect your data. Nevertheless, it is important to note that no system is impervious to security breaches. We are devoted to staying ahead of potential threats to your data security.


7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for, nor directed towards, children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or retain information related to individuals under 13 years of age.


8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is subject to updates and refinements to stay current with evolving laws and technologies. We will notify you of substantial changes.
