Partnerships


How to become Partner?

In the realm of community Discord servers, partnerships stand as integral conduits for expedited growth and enhanced outreach. Recognizing their paramount importance, Team RedFox extends an invitation for collaborative opportunities on our Discord server.

To initiate the process, kindly join our server here, enable the "view all channels" function, then navigate to the dedicated "start-collaboration" channel located in the main category. Here, we encourage you to furnish us with essential details about your server. Include insights such as membership statistics, the nature of your server (communal or specialized), frequency of member activity, the thematic focus of your server, and its primary language.

What to do after Partnership is confirmed?

Upon successful approval of your partnership request, the owner or administrator of our server will facilitate the seamless integration of our promotional text into your server's advertising channel. It is imperative that this content remains unaltered.

Furthermore, as a recognized partner, you will be conferred the distinguished "Partner" role. This designation bestows upon you the privilege to periodically share hourly advertisements in the designated "collaboration-servers" channel, housed within the overarching "main" category. Your active participation in this shared space will enhance visibility and engagement, contributing to a mutually beneficial partnership.
